Welcome to Toilet Diamond Bowl

Rate each college football bowl game from 1 point to the total number of games. The player with the highest total points wins!

How It Works

  1. Sign up with a valid access key to create your profile
  2. Go to "Point Submission" to assign points to each bowl game
  3. Assign points from 1 (least valuable) to N (most valuable), where N is the total number of games
  4. Each point value can only be used once
  5. After games are played, your score is the sum of points you assigned to winning teams
  6. Go to "CFP Bracket" to pick the winners of the College Football Playoff
  7. Check "Standings" to see how you rank
